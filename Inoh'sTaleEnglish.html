Inoh's Tale


Inoh’s Tale is a Zelda-Like with references such as “Castle in the Sky” or Avatar, the last airbender . This game uses the old mechanics of the first Zelda such as Link’s awakening in a more nervous and dynamic way in order to modernize them. The player will have to use both reflexion and control of his power to survive through this world. He will have to find clues about the mysterious old goddess to save his town form the devil cult of Terenos.

This game was developed in 5 months during the 2020 French containment by 6 students. I did all the programming of the project and most of the graphics integration.

Skills acquired :

During this project I learn the basics of C# serialization and I improved my Gameplay Programming skills.
I also worked on the communication with the artists in order to do the asset’s integration.
