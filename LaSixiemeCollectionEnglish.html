La Sixième Collection

In a world where appearance leads the society, the “grands créateurs” are like kings, and the one above them all is call “Maitre de la mode”. La Sixième Collection is a board game in wich the players are 4 “grands créateurs” who wants to become the “Maitre de la mode”. To do so, they have to create new style by combining the clothes cards and robe the other’s popularity points with them “atouts”.

Description :

La Sixième Collection was created during a school projet by 5 students. Then we decided to work more on it and now the game will be published in few weeks by Victoria Games.

Skills acquired :

I worked as a Game Designer on this game. I had to learn management to work with the team. I also work on my documentations skills for the project and learn how to follow constraints (the game had to be one hour long maximum and need to be for a familial public).

I also managed all the publishing rights of the project and had to deal with all the paperwork to do so.
