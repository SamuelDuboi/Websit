Péché A La Ligne

In Péchés A La Ligne, you play a priest who has to choose between absolve or condemn souls. To make your choices, you need to follow your god orders. This game was made in 48 hours during a game jam made to welcome new students. I had to manage the freshman and teach them the basics of game development.

Skills acquired :

JI managed the team in the development of a basic idea knowing the skills of the team and the really short time. I took part of the conception and the development of the Core GamePlay and the interface of the game.
