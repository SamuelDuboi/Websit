STA.R.




STA.R. is a Augmented Reality game playable on mobile. In this game, the player has to complete constellations that appear on plane surfaces such as tables or walls. To do so, he has to take the floating stars and bring them back to the good position on the constellation. The player will discover his environment to hunt stars et solve the puzzles of the constellations.



Skills acquired :

I learn to use a Augmenter Reality in game with the AR Core of Unity.

It was also my first experience of mobile game development so I had to learn the build testing of mobile devices on Unity. I also learn to optimize my code for better game performances.
