Keep The Beat en Festival

Random bazar contacted us to expose Keep The Beat for two months at Connexion : an exhibition in parallel of the Fantastic Film‘s European Festival from the 9th of September to the 30th of October 2021.






Escape Time

Escape Time is a puzzle game produced in 4 months by 8 students. It uses the main principles of Escape Games while adding mechanics inspired by its platform like turning the room upside down to discover what is on in roof with a swipe.
