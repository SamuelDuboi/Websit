Inoh's Tale

Inoh's Tale is a pixel art game, with Zelda-like made during a 6 months school project.
The game uses Zelda’s mechanics in a more nervous and dynamic way. In a devested world where island drift in the sky, follow the clues that your family left for you and save your town from the devil cult of Terenos.
